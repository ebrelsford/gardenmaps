How to use this site Select items from the right side bar that you would like to view. The first one you click will fill in the yellow box, and the second will fill in the red box. The orange box will show you any overlap. To change which items you are comparing, click the in the top right corner of the yellow or red box.

For example, if you click art, then mural, all surveyed gardens with murals will be highlighted with yellow. Then, if you click volunteers followed by hosts volunteers, all surveyed gardens that host volunteers will be highlighted in red. All surveyed gardens with murals that also host volunteers will appear in orange. If you now decide that you are no longer interested in murals, you can click the next to 'mural' on the left.
